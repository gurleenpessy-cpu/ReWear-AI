Our Story

A legacy of sweetness since 1985

🪔 मिठाई मंदिर was founded by Shri Raghunath Prasad in 1985 with a simple dream — to bring the authentic taste of traditional Indian sweets to every household. What started as a small shop in the heart of the city has now become a beloved brand trusted by thousands of families across generations.

Every sweet we make is crafted using time-honored recipes, 100% pure desi ghee, and the freshest ingredients sourced directly from trusted farms. Our master halwais (sweet makers) bring decades of experience and passion to every batch they prepare.

From grand weddings to intimate family celebrations, from festive seasons to everyday indulgences — मिठाई मंदिर has been a part of millions of sweet moments. 🙏
